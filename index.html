Know about

Dr. Sudipta Dutta

It is the sole aim of us to provide total take-care of a patient in his lifetime Diabetes journey…......For years, patients have depended on me to provide this valuable service. I offer this and other specialized options to anyone in need of a Diabetologist they can trust. I’m Dr. Sudipta Dutta, MBBS, MD (Diabetologist), and I’m dedicated to building long-lasting relationships based on trust and medical integrity with every single patient.Not only treatment, awareness about different aspect of this disease is equally important. Due to Carbohydrate rich diet, sedentary lifestyle and bad genes, peoples of south east Asians are more susceptible to develop Diabetes at an early ages.....to combat or prevent, not only medicine but an comprehensive approach including lifestyle changes, proper diet guideline and all-round take care is very necessary…This sought of Care whatever You Deserve through individual and personalized way is our foremost priority as a Diabetes care team.

Why consulting with me???


When you decide to put your health in my hands, it immediately becomes my number one priority. I’ve been serving the community as a Diabetologist since 2017. I value the relationships, I form with each patient, and believe that goes a long way in improving their health care outcomes. Give me a chance to find out how I can help you today. Your health is my top priority I am being a doctor also in a fight with you, always, against Diabetes!!!PROMISE...
To me, nothing matters more than your health and comfort, which is why I’ve put in so much time and effort into advancing my medical practice and techniques. Please get in touch to schedule an initial consultation.
